About me

Hello there! I am very pleased to meet you here, on my handmade online resume. Please have a seat and let me tell you a few words about where I came from. My name is Ewelina and I’m in the middle of a difficult and time absorbing (but yet exciting!) process of becoming a skilled and experienced Frontend Developer. Since 2008 I had worked in the fashion industry as a fashion writer of several mainstream glossy magazines. But in 2016 I decided to make a major change in my professional life and just quit. I needed time and space to approach new world of code – something I have dreamt of doing for years but never had found a courage. After ripping a lot of my hair off and asking myself 'why on Earth did I get into that' I can definitely say that it was the best decision of my life. But let's get straight to the point. The website I have a pleasure to host you on have been created with using plain HTML5, Bootstrap and some simple jQuery. My aim is to have both solid foundations and knowledge of modern technologies such as Angular2 framework. If you agree to share your precious experience and teach me – in return I can offer my hard work, commitment and excitement. Let's get in touch! Show more
